Mission

At JavaSeek, our mission is to provide an engaging and interactive platform for learners of all levels to enhance their JavaScript skills. We believe that learning should be fun and accessible, making coding a rewarding experience for everyone.

Who Is JavaSeek

JavaSeek was founded by Sarah Schwarz, a student of the NCAT Web Systems Class. It is a creative, simple game designed to entice learning about JavaScript in real-time. JavaSeek is still in mid-production but will have its final release date in December 2024..

Join Us

Whether you're just starting out or looking to refine your skills, JavaSeek provides the resources you need to succeed. Join our community today and take your JavaScript knowledge to the next level!
